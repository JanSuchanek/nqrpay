# nqrpay

Czech QR Platba (SPD) — QR code payment generator for Czech/Slovak banks.

## Key Classes
- `QrPayment` — generates SPD string and QR image

## Conventions
- PHP 8.2+ with `declare(strict_types=1)`
- PSR-4 autoloading in `src/`
- Nette DI Extension for service registration
- PHPStan level 9
- Part of `jansuchanek/*` on Packagist
- GitHub: https://github.com/JanSuchanek/nqrpay
